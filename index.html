About ICT Authority Partnership

The digitalent program owes its success through the public-private partnership (ppp) model. We leverage on this partnership to ensure program objectives are achieved as most of the components support is sourced from our partners.

What is Partnernship?

A partnership is an arrangement where parties, known as business partners, agree to cooperate to advance their mutual interests. The partners in a partnership may be individuals, businesses, interest-based organizations, schools, governments or combinations.
